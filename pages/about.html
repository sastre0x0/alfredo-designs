Let's work together!

Design by the hour:

Bring the skills and resources of Alfredo Designs to your project to bring to reality your vision, as edited by us. We offer an hourly rate for DIY clients who don't mind doing some of the legwork, from design consultations to complete and detailed design plans, including color and furniture selection, this is the ideal option for clients seeking spectacular design with more independent execution.

Fixed fee design:

For projects requiring a more across-the-board approach, Alfredo Designs offers full-service concierge level fixed fee options. Perfect for multi-room renovations, ground-up construction projects or a full-sweep remodel, fixed fee pricing brings the skills of an experienced interior design professional to your home with a turnkey process. Specifically tailored to the needs of each client, this package includes not only complete interior design services, but brings space planning, furnishing sourcing, project management, installation and post-installation services to the mix.

Design your own program:

Perhaps your project lies somewhere between hourly and fixed fee pricing. Not a problem: Alfredo Designs can structure a program as unique and individual as the interiors we create. We love to collaborate, so feel free to share your vision with us!

Not sure which option works best for you? Our initial consultation is gratis, so feel free to contact us at (949)295-0557 or Alfredo@AlfredoDesigns.com
